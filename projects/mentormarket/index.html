Mentors will publicize how they choose to communicate with mentees. They will set realistic expectations for the level of assistance they're willing to provide.

By placing limits on the duration of the mentorship period and the number of relationships a user can maintain in a given time period, MentorMarket plans to foster quality over quantity mentoring in its connections and communications.

Value

MentorMarket's rating and compensation system will hold users accountable for the quality of their contributions. A marketplace where those offering the greatest value recieve the greatest benefits.
